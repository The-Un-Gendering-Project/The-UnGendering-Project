Kalkieh on designing the logo :

"The logo primarily consists of purple which is made by mixing pink and blue together- the two colours representing the stereotype of the gender binary. The face doesn't reflect on a particular gender and is open to interpretation; while the patches of colour inside are to represent the multiplicity of ways a person may express themselves regardless of the gender they are from"
